Computer Science Department

Welcome to the Computer Science Department. Here, we focus on the development of software and hardware systems that enable us to solve complex problems efficiently. Our courses cover a wide range of topics, including programming, data structures, algorithms,and artificial intelligence.

Our Courses

  • Introduction to Programming
  • Data Structures and Algorithms
  • Operating Systems
  • Artificial Intelligence

Our Faculty

Our faculty members are experts in their respective fields and are dedicated to providing the best possible education to our students. They are always available to help you with your questions and concerns.

Our Research

Our research team is constantly working on cutting-edge projects that aim to push the boundaries of what is possible in the field of computer science. Some of our recent projects include:

  • Developing a new programming language for quantum computers
  • Creating a machine learning model that can predict stock prices with high accuracy
  • Building a system that can automatically detect and remove hate speech from social media platforms
